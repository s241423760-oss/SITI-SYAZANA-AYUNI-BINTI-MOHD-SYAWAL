Role & Contribution 🐾

Impact and Responsibilities of the Founder

Role

As the manager of Bustana Kucing, Mohd Taufik is responsible for overseeing daily operations, including managing staff, coordinating cat care, and ensuring proper maintenance of facilities.

He leads a team of 27 staff members and manages a large facility that includes multiple wards such as ICU, recovery ward, and mother-and-kitten ward.

Contribution

  • Providing shelter for more than 2,000 stray cats
  • Ensuring food, medical care, and protection for animals
  • Raising awareness about animal welfare in the community
  • Collaborating with professional veterinary clinics

Collaboration

Mohd Taufik works closely with veterinary clinics such as Klinik Haiwan Purrfect, Klinik Haiwan Sanctuar, and Klinik Haiwan Tuah to ensure proper healthcare and treatment for the cats.
