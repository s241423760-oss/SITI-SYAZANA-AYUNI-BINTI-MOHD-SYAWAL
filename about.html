About Mohd Taufik 🐱

Founder of Bustana Kucing

Background

Mohd Taufik Bin Md Zain is the founder and manager of Bustana Kucing, officially established in March 2015 and registered under ROS in July 2018.

He founded Kelab Bustana Kucing Malaysia with the aim of protecting and caring for stray cats in Perlis and surrounding areas.

With the support of co-founders Noor Azlina Binti Abu Bakar, Dr Thomas Jong Ah Khaw, and Haixiey Majibon, the organisation continues to grow and serve the community.

Bustana Kucing is also licensed under the Animal Welfare Act 2015 (Act 772) by the Department of Veterinary Services Malaysia.

Vision

The vision of Bustana Kucing is to become a leading animal welfare organisation in Malaysia by providing proper care, shelter, and medical support for abandoned cats.
